## AI Infrastructure

Companies building the tools, platforms, and plumbing that make AI systems work.

**[Braintrust](https://www.braintrust.dev)** — Platform for evaluating, testing, and monitoring AI applications.  Helps teams ship AI products with confidence by providing robust evaluation frameworks.

**[Bespoke Labs](https://www.bespokelabs.ai)** — Tools for high-quality synthetic data generation and post-training optimization, enabling teams to improve model performance without massive real-world data collection.

**[Cake AI](https://www.cake.ai)** — Making it easy to deploy and manage open-source AI models in production, reducing the infrastructure burden for teams that want flexibility without vendor lock-in.

**[CocoIndex](https://cocoindex.io)** — Open-source ETL framework purpose-built for AI data pipelines, helping teams move and transform data for model training and inference.

**Harmonic Labs** — Building ViolinOS, an inference control plane that optimizes how AI models are served in production, improving performance and cost efficiency.

**[Vals AI](https://www.vals.ai)** — Automated compliance and safety testing for AI models, helping companies ensure their models meet regulatory and internal policy requirements.

**[Podonos](https://www.podonos.com)** — Human-in-the-loop evaluation platform for voice AI, with a global network of evaluators that deliver quality assessments in under 12 hours.

**[CopyCat](https://www.runcopycat.com)** — AI-powered browser automation agents that can observe and replicate human workflows, bridging the gap between AI and everyday computer tasks.

**[Wafer.ai](https://www.wafer.ai)** — Applying AI to semiconductor design, optimizing chip layouts and performance.  Sits at the intersection of AI and hardware.

## Vertical AI Applications

Companies applying AI to transform specific industries.

**[Klarity](https://www.klarity.ai)** — AI-powered revenue recognition and compliance automation.  Takes the manual work out of accounting close processes for finance teams.

**[Overview AI](https://www.overview.ai)** — Computer vision for manufacturing quality control.  Automates visual inspection on production lines, catching defects that human inspectors miss.

**[Pauling AI](https://www.pauling.ai)** — Using AI to accelerate drug discovery, helping pharmaceutical researchers identify promising compounds and predict molecular interactions faster.

**[Neru Health](https://www.neruhealth.com)** — AI-powered platform for chronic condition management, starting with sleep therapy.  Combines clinical protocols with AI to scale care delivery.

**[GoodCall](https://www.goodcall.com)** — AI-powered phone answering service for SMBs that handles customer calls, takes messages, and routes inquiries — so small business owners can focus on their work.

**[Conversant AI](https://www.conversant.ai)** — AI for the industrial distribution market — automating quoting, product compatibility, and supplier onboarding for businesses that deal in complex physical products.

**[Diplo AI](https://diplo-ai.com)** — AI that helps automotive companies navigate and comply with evolving regulatory requirements, automating the analysis of complex regulation documents.

**[Trisetra AI](https://trisetra.com)** — AI-powered home design that generates 3D, code-compliant architectural plans, making the design process faster and more accessible.

**[Aidaptive](https://aidaptive.com)** — AI-driven personalization engine for eCommerce and hospitality, predicting customer preferences to improve conversion and experience.

## Developer Productivity

Companies helping developers and knowledge workers be more effective.

**[Blue (HeyBlue)](https://heyblue.com)** — A voice-first AI agent that can operate your computer — navigate apps, fill out forms, and execute workflows through natural conversation.

**[Pally](https://www.pally.com)** — AI-powered CRM and relationship intelligence, helping people maintain and nurture their professional networks.

**[Thesis Labs](https://thesislabs.ai)** — Tools for accelerating research workflows, helping researchers find, synthesize, and build on existing knowledge more efficiently.

**[Vybe.build](https://www.vybe.build)** — An AI-powered platform for building applications, lowering the barrier to turning ideas into working software.
