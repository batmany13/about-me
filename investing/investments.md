## AI Infrastructure

Companies building the tools, platforms, and plumbing that make AI systems work.

**Braintrust** — Platform for evaluating, testing, and monitoring AI applications.  Helps teams ship AI products with confidence by providing robust evaluation frameworks.

**Bespoke Labs** — Tools for high-quality synthetic data generation and post-training optimization, enabling teams to improve model performance without massive real-world data collection.

**Cake AI** — Making it easy to deploy and manage open-source AI models in production, reducing the infrastructure burden for teams that want flexibility without vendor lock-in.

**CocoIndex** — Open-source ETL framework purpose-built for AI data pipelines, helping teams move and transform data for model training and inference.

**Harmonic Labs** — Building ViolinOS, an inference control plane that optimizes how AI models are served in production, improving performance and cost efficiency.

**Vals AI** — Automated compliance and safety testing for AI models, helping companies ensure their models meet regulatory and internal policy requirements.

**Podonos** — Human-in-the-loop evaluation platform for voice AI, with a global network of evaluators that deliver quality assessments in under 12 hours.

**CopyCat** — AI-powered browser automation agents that can observe and replicate human workflows, bridging the gap between AI and everyday computer tasks.

**Wafer.ai** — Applying AI to semiconductor design, optimizing chip layouts and performance.  Sits at the intersection of AI and hardware.

## Vertical AI Applications

Companies applying AI to transform specific industries.

**Klarity** — AI-powered revenue recognition and compliance automation.  Takes the manual work out of accounting close processes for finance teams.

**Overview AI** — Computer vision for manufacturing quality control.  Automates visual inspection on production lines, catching defects that human inspectors miss.

**Pauling AI** — Using AI to accelerate drug discovery, helping pharmaceutical researchers identify promising compounds and predict molecular interactions faster.

**Neru Health** — AI-powered platform for chronic condition management, starting with sleep therapy.  Combines clinical protocols with AI to scale care delivery.

**GoodCall** — AI-powered phone answering service for SMBs that handles customer calls, takes messages, and routes inquiries — so small business owners can focus on their work.

**LegalOn** — AI-assisted contract review platform that helps legal teams review, negotiate, and manage contracts faster and more accurately.

**Conversant AI** — AI for the industrial distribution market — automating quoting, product compatibility, and supplier onboarding for businesses that deal in complex physical products.

**Diplo AI** — AI that helps automotive companies navigate and comply with evolving regulatory requirements, automating the analysis of complex regulation documents.

**Trisetra AI** — AI-powered home design that generates 3D, code-compliant architectural plans, making the design process faster and more accessible.

**Aidaptive** — AI-driven personalization engine for eCommerce and hospitality, predicting customer preferences to improve conversion and experience.

## Developer Productivity

Companies helping developers and knowledge workers be more effective.

**Blue (HeyBlue)** — A voice-first AI agent that can operate your computer — navigate apps, fill out forms, and execute workflows through natural conversation.

**Pally** — AI-powered CRM and relationship intelligence, helping people maintain and nurture their professional networks.

**Thesis Labs** — Tools for accelerating research workflows, helping researchers find, synthesize, and build on existing knowledge more efficiently.

**Vybe.build** — An AI-powered platform for building applications, lowering the barrier to turning ideas into working software.

## Other

**ChainML** — Building infrastructure for decentralized AI agents, exploring the intersection of AI and decentralized computing.

**AlphaXIV** — Adding a community discussion and annotation layer on top of academic papers, making research more accessible and collaborative.

**Koolio.ai** — AI-powered podcast editing and production tools that automate the tedious parts of creating podcast content.

**DeepVista** — AI agent focused on business communication, helping teams communicate more effectively with customers and partners.
