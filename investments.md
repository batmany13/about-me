# AI Fund Portfolio

As a seed-stage investor, I focus on AI companies across infrastructure, vertical applications, and developer productivity.  Below are the companies I've invested in through the fund.  For my investment philosophy, see [thesis](thesis.md).

## AI Infrastructure

Companies building the tools, platforms, and plumbing that make AI systems work.

**Braintrust** — AI Observability & Evaluation
Platform for evaluating, testing, and monitoring AI applications.  Helps teams ship AI products with confidence by providing robust evaluation frameworks.

**Bespoke Labs** — Synthetic Data & Post-Training
Building tools for high-quality synthetic data generation and post-training optimization, enabling teams to improve model performance without massive real-world data collection.

**Cake AI** — Managed Open-Source AI Infrastructure
Making it easy to deploy and manage open-source AI models in production, reducing the infrastructure burden for teams that want flexibility without vendor lock-in.

**CocoIndex** — AI Data Pipelines
Open-source ETL framework purpose-built for AI data pipelines, helping teams move and transform data for model training and inference.

**Harmonic Labs** — Inference Control Plane
Building ViolinOS, an inference control plane that optimizes how AI models are served in production, improving performance and cost efficiency.

**Vals AI** — AI Model Compliance Testing
Automated compliance and safety testing for AI models, helping companies ensure their models meet regulatory and internal policy requirements.

**Podonos** — Voice AI Evaluation
Human-in-the-loop evaluation platform for voice AI, with a global network of evaluators that deliver quality assessments in under 12 hours.

**CopyCat** — AI Browser Agents
Building AI-powered browser automation agents that can observe and replicate human workflows, bridging the gap between AI and everyday computer tasks.

**Wafer.ai** — AI Chip Design Optimization
Applying AI to semiconductor design, optimizing chip layouts and performance.  Sits at the intersection of AI and hardware.

## Vertical AI Applications

Companies applying AI to transform specific industries.

**Klarity** — Accounting & Compliance Automation
AI-powered revenue recognition and compliance automation.  Takes the manual work out of accounting close processes for finance teams.

**Overview AI** — Manufacturing Vision Inspection
Computer vision for manufacturing quality control.  Automates visual inspection on production lines, catching defects that human inspectors miss.

**Pauling AI** — AI Drug Discovery
Using AI to accelerate drug discovery, helping pharmaceutical researchers identify promising compounds and predict molecular interactions faster.

**Neru Health** — Chronic Care & Sleep Therapy
AI-powered platform for chronic condition management, starting with sleep therapy.  Combines clinical protocols with AI to scale care delivery.

**GoodCall** — AI Phone Agent for Small Businesses
An AI-powered phone answering service for SMBs that handles customer calls, takes messages, and routes inquiries — so small business owners can focus on their work.

**LegalOn** — AI Contract Review
AI-assisted contract review platform that helps legal teams review, negotiate, and manage contracts faster and more accurately.

**Conversant AI** — Industrial Product Intelligence
AI for the industrial distribution market — automating quoting, product compatibility, and supplier onboarding for businesses that deal in complex physical products.

**Diplo AI** — Automotive Regulatory Compliance
AI that helps automotive companies navigate and comply with evolving regulatory requirements, automating the analysis of complex regulation documents.

**Trisetra AI** — AI Home Design
AI-powered home design that generates 3D, code-compliant architectural plans, making the design process faster and more accessible.

**Aidaptive** — Predictive Personalization for eCommerce
AI-driven personalization engine for eCommerce and hospitality, predicting customer preferences to improve conversion and experience.
