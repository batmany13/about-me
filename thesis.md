# Startup Advisory and Investment Thesis

I invest at the seed stage in AI companies.  My thesis has been shaped by a decade of operating at Netflix scale, building startups from scratch, and advising founders across different stages.

## Why AI, Why Now

We're in a generational platform shift.  AI is not just a feature — it's reshaping how software gets built, how businesses operate, and how entire industries function.  I believe the best time to invest in AI is now, while the infrastructure is still being built and vertical applications are just beginning to find product-market fit.

## What I Look For

### The Problem Has to Be Real

I'm drawn to companies solving problems that exist today, not speculative future needs.  The best AI companies I've seen take a painful, manual, or broken process and make it dramatically better.  If you have to convince someone they have the problem, it's probably not the right problem.

### AI Should Be the Moat, Not Just a Feature

I look for companies where AI capabilities create a compounding advantage — through data flywheels, proprietary training data, or domain-specific models that get better with use.  Slapping an LLM wrapper on an existing workflow is not a thesis-fit company.

### Founding Teams That Can Execute

This is where my [people leadership background](managing.md) shapes my investing.  I pay close attention to:
- **Technical depth** — can the founders actually build what they're pitching?
- **Domain expertise** — do they deeply understand the industry they're targeting?
- **Team dynamics** — do they complement each other?  Can they hire well?
- **Resilience** — have they been through hard things before?

### Sectors I Focus On

- **AI Infrastructure** — the picks-and-shovels of the AI era: evaluation, data pipelines, inference optimization, compliance testing
- **Vertical AI Applications** — AI applied to specific industries (healthcare, legal, manufacturing, industrial) where domain knowledge creates defensibility
- **Developer Productivity** — tools that make developers and knowledge workers meaningfully more productive with AI

## How I Help

I'm not a passive investor.  What I offer founders:

- **Technical architecture feedback** — I can go deep on system design, API architecture, and scaling challenges
- **Leadership and hiring advice** — building the right team and culture early is the highest-leverage thing a founder can do
- **Pattern matching from Netflix and startups** — what works at scale, what breaks, and what the transition from 0-to-1 to 1-to-N actually looks like
- **Honest, direct feedback** — I'd rather be the one to tell you something isn't working than have you learn it from the market
